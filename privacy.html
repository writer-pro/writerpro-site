PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: January 20, 2026

We collect only what we need to run Writer Pro and keep your data secure.

Summary

We store account info, preferences, and credit balance. Payment data stays with Stripe. You can request deletion anytime.

1. Data We Collect

We collect account email, name, app preferences, credit balance, and subscription status. Payment data is handled by Stripe.

2. How We Use Data

We use data to authenticate you, manage credits, deliver features, prevent abuse, and provide support.

3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by Stripe. We never store full card numbers or security codes.

4. Data Storage

Account data is stored in Supabase. We use encryption in transit and apply access controls to protect it.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to run the app (e.g., Stripe, Supabase). We do not sell your data.

6. Retention

We retain account data while your account is active. You can request deletion in the billing page; we retain minimal records if required by law.

7. Your Choices

You can update your account details, reset your password, or request deletion through the website.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage and cookies to keep you signed in and protect account limits. We do not use third‑party ad trackers.

9. International Users

Your data may be processed in regions where our service providers operate. We take steps to keep it secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions? Contact us through the Support page on the website.
